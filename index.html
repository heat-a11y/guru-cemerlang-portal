The Ground — Cohesive Narrative

From low mastery → independent proficiency

Final Narrative Output

“My most memorable holiday was when I visited Langkawi with my family during the school break. It happened in December last year. The crystal-clear waters and golden beaches made it feel like paradise. More importantly, this trip taught me the value of spending quality time with loved ones. I learned that happiness is not about grand adventures but about the simple moments shared with family.”

💡 Pedagogical Insight: The Raindrop Method guides students from broad unstructured ideas (Cloud) through WH-question scaffolding (Raindrops) using Computational Thinking decomposition, producing coherent narratives (Ground) — systematically building independent writing proficiency.
